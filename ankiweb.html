About the Add-on

The Migaku Anki Add-on is a combination of all our previous addons and their features. But it is not only a combination, we also simplified the set up and usage of the add-on to make it more accessible.

Important: In order to simplify the add-ons usage and setup, some functions were moved to the Migaku Browser Extension and are now handled from there. If you don't have the Migaku Browser Extension installed, they will not work. Below, those features are marked as such. If you want to know more about the Migaku Browser Extension you can watch a summary of its most important features here.

General Features

Migaku Editor
  • In-place Editing
    • Editing without having to open the separate editor window
  • Persistent Editor Window
    • Editor Window stays open and is automatically filled with the next card info while you review. Infos in the editor window are hidden until the card is turned around.
Migaku Retirement
  • Set a retirement interval and control what happens to cards in each deck once they have reached that interval
    • A user may choose to delete all of their retired cards, add tags to the cards, suspend the cards, move those cards to a "retirement deck," or do any combination of those options.
Migaku Vacation (will be implemented soon)
  • Schedule vacations away from reviews
  • Optimize and smooth daily review counts
  • Taking a "Sick Day" and pushing all reviews one day into the future
  • Catching up slowly on overdue reviews using the “Catch-Up” feature
  • Studying cards that will be due in the future using the “Get-Ahead” feature
  • Maintain card ease
Migaku Flexible Grading
  • Pass/Fail
    • Reduces Ankis 4 buttons to 2, Pass and Fail.
  • Maintain Ease Factor
    • Upon failing a card, the ease factor of that card will remain the same.
Migaku Dictionary (Migaku Browser Extension required)
  • Look up words in multiple dictionaries at once. In Anki, as well as globally
  • Add definitions, images, Forvo audio and example sentences to cards with one click

Language Specific Features (Migaku Browser Extension required)

  • Play audio of all words by simply clicking on them

  • Add pitch coloring to all words
  • Automatically add readings
  • Pitch graphes on hover

  • Add tone coloring to all words
  • Add Jyutping for Cantonese
  • Add Pinyin or Zhuyin for Mandarin
  • Convert all characters to the simplified or traditional variant

  • Show part of speech and the dictionary from of the word on hover (to be implemented)

  • Show part of speech and the dictionary from of the word on hover (to be implemented)
Spanish
  • Add gender coloring to all words
  • Show part of speech and the dictionary from of the word on hover (to be implemented)
Portuguese

  • Show part of speech and the dictionary from of the word on hover (to be implemented)

How to install the Addon

If you haven't installed Anki yet, you can download it here.

After you are done installing Anki, open it. Next, navigate to |Tools|>|Add-ons|.

In the window that opens, press |Get Add-ons…|.

In the window that opens, enter the code at the end of this page and press |OK|.

After the installation is finished, restart Anki and you are done!
